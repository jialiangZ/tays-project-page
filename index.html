TaYS enables real-time streaming Chain-of-Thought reasoning for large vision-language models.

Abstract

Large Vision-Language Models (LVLMs) struggle with streaming video reasoning due to memory and latency constraints. We propose Think-as-You-See (TaYS), a streaming Chain-of-Thought framework that incrementally processes video frames while maintaining reasoning consistency. TaYS introduces dual KV-cache and decoupled positional encoding for efficient long-context modeling. Experiments demonstrate strong improvements in both efficiency and reasoning quality.

Highlights

  • 🔥 First streaming Chain-of-Thought framework for LVLMs
  • ⚡ Real-time reasoning without waiting for full video
  • 🧠 Efficient dual KV-cache design
  • 📈 Strong performance on long-video reasoning tasks
